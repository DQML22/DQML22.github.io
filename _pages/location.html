Conference and accomodation

The conference will be held in Hintertux, in the Tyrol region of Austria.
The structure hosting the conference, which is also the accomodation, is the Alpenbad Hotel Hohenhaus, an hotel situated away from the high street but still in a central location in Hintertux. Offering the best view of the imposing Hintertux Glacier, the Hohenhaus is located in a uniquely beautiful, sunny, and quiet spot.
The address is Hohenhausgasse 774, 6294 Hintertux, Austria
