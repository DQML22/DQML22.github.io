Conference and accomodation

The conference will be held in Hintertux, in the Tyrol region of Austria.
The structure hosting the conference, which is also the accomodation, is the Alpenbad Hotel Hohenhaus, an hotel situated away from the high street but still in a central location in Hintertux.
The address is Hohenhausgasse 774, 6294 Hintertux, Austria


Travel details from Munich

A bus will leave from Munich airport to the conference location and will bring us back from the hotel to Munich airport. Here are the details:

  • 01/02/2022: admission starts from 11:00 a.m.. The bus will leave at 11:30 am from Munich airport to Hintertux, Hotel Hohenhaus.
  • 04/02/2022: admission starts from 11:15 a.m. The bus will leave at 11:30 a.m. from Hintertux, Hotel Hohenhaus to Munich airport.

Leisure activities

The hotel is located 3 min from the skiing slope and a cross-country skiing trail. There will be a free transportation called “Tuxer Sportbus” from the hotel to the slope the whole morning.
From 14.30-20.00 every full and half our a bus is going back and forth from the hotel to “Hohenhaus Tenne”.
Skiing tickets can be bought at the hotel. There is no ticket needed for cross-country skiing. (Cross-country) Skiing and snowboarding equipment can be rent at Sports Tenner directly at the valley station. https://bit.ly/3o4nDJR
There is also the option for ice skating and ice stock sports. The location is Lanersbach and the bus also goes there.
